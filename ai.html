Modern creative workflow

Traditional artistic foundations.
Modern production systems.

After more than a decade of traditional visual production, I embraced AI as an extension of my creative workflow, not as a replacement for creativity.

My background in game art, illustration, branding and visual design allows me to direct AI with intention instead of relying on it for solutions. Every project combines artistic experience, creative judgment and modern production tools into a single workflow.

From concept exploration and image generation to compositing, motion design, sound, editing and final delivery, every stage is carefully directed, refined and quality-controlled.

Technology accelerates production.

Creative direction remains human.

The workflow

  1. Concept

    Build an idea that can technically work with the available AI production toolkit.

  2. Images

    Generate and iterate source images in Firefly or DaVinci until they are ready for animated video.

  3. Scenes

    Prompt the selected images in Veo 3.1, iterate the motion and build every scene.

  4. Audio

    Create music in Suno, sound FX in Runway and voiceover in ElevenLabs.

  5. Final Edit

    Bring every scene and audio layer into Adobe After Effects, then edit and polish the full video.
